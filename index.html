About Nawa Nawa

Dedicated Leader for Matero

Hon. Nawa Nawa, also known as "Ba Chair", is a passionate leader committed to transforming Matero. Born and raised in Lusaka, he understands the challenges and opportunities within the constituency.

With a background in accountancy from ZICA and local business experience, Nawa Nawa brings both professional expertise and community understanding to his vision for Matero.

My Vision for Matero

Better Jobs & Economic Growth

Creating sustainable employment opportunities through local business support, vocational training, and attracting investment to Matero. Empowering entrepreneurs and creating a thriving local economy.

Quality Healthcare for All

Improving healthcare facilities and access to medical services for all residents of Matero, with special focus on maternal and child health, and preventive care programs.

Modern Infrastructure

Upgrading roads, drainage systems, water supply, and electricity to enhance quality of life and economic activity in Matero. Ensuring every community has basic amenities.

Empowered Youth & Education

Providing skills training, educational support, and recreational facilities to unlock the potential of Matero's youth population. Investing in the next generation of leaders.
